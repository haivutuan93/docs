- Present
	+ Present simple: S + V
	+ Present complete: S + have + V_ed
	+ Present continous: S + is + V_ing
	+ Present complete continous: S + have been + V_ing
- Past:
	+ Past simple: S + V_ed
	+ Past complete: S + had + V_ed
	+ Past continous: S + was + V_ing
	+ Past complete continous: S + had been + V_ing
- Future:
	+ Future simple: S + will/be going to + V						
	+ Future complete: S + will + have + V_ed
	+ Future continous: S + will + is + V_ing
	+ Future complete continous: S + will + had been + V_ing
